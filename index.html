Simple Process

How It Works

Get your barangay documents in 3 easy steps — no need to visit the hall until your document is ready.

1
📝
Create an Account
Register with your name, email, and mobile number. It only takes 2 minutes to get started.
2
📄
Submit Your Request
Choose the document you need, fill in your details, and pay the fee online via GCash or Maya.
3
🔔
Get Notified
We'll send you a notification when your document is ready. Just visit the barangay hall to claim it.
4
🏛️
Claim Your Document
Bring a valid government ID to the barangay hall. Your document will be ready and waiting for you.

Why BarangayConnect

Everything You Need

A complete digital system for barangay document services — built for residents and staff alike.

📱
Works on Any Device
Access from your phone, tablet, or computer. No app download needed — just open your browser.
💳
Online Payment
Pay securely via GCash or Maya. No need to bring cash — just send money and enter your reference number.
🔔
Real-Time Notifications
Get notified instantly when your request is approved, processed, and ready for pickup.
🔍
Track Your Request
Use your reference number to check the status of your request anytime, even without logging in.
🛡️
Admin Dashboard
Barangay staff get a full dashboard to manage requests, residents, reports, and settings.
📊
Monthly Reports
Automatic monthly summaries with total requests, fees collected, and processing analytics.
